Learn about A&T Sip N' Bite Shack

In the heart of General Trias Cavite, nestled near the bustling Malabon Plaza, lies a simple yet cozy culinary haven known as A&T Snack N' Bite Shack. Founded and established on a crisp February day in 2023, this unassuming eatery quickly became a beloved fixture in the community. Founded by students Angel and Thea, their passion for cooking and hospitality created a warm haven. With family recipes and innovative flavors, they crafted a menu of savory and sweet delights, using fresh, local ingredients. Beyond the food, it was the genuine hospitality and sense of community that made it a beloved spot. Over the years, A&T Snack N' Bite Shack flourished, becoming a cherished gathering place for friends and families, embodying Angel and Thea's commitment to spreading joy through food and hospitality.


Our Mission & Vision

TAt A&T Snack N' Bite Shack, we're more than just a restaurant. We're a welcoming hub where every bite feels like home and every moment becomes a cherished memory. Our mission is to provide a vibrant space where friends, partners, and families can gather to enjoy delicious food, genuine hospitality, and create lasting connections. We're committed to crafting flavorful dishes with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients and delivering exceptional service with warmth and sincerity. Our vision is to be an integral part of the community, where everyone feels valued and welcomed, and where every visit is an opportunity for culinary delight and genuine connection. With our focus on sustainability and supporting local suppliers, we strive to be the go-to destination for unforgettable hangouts and heartwarming moments.
